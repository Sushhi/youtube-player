YouTube Player

A simple embed YouTube Player with custom controls

Controls

Pressing 'Space' toggle 'Play' / 'Pause'.
Pressing 'Left' / 'Right' arrow move to 'Previous' / 'Next' frame.
Pressing 'Up' / 'Down' arrow move 1 second 'Backward' / 'Forward'.
